## 📊 Implications for the Broad Stock Market

- **Rate-sensitive sectors face the most binary risk.** If the easing bias is removed and dots shift up, rate-sensitive groups (real estate, utilities, small caps/Russell 2000) tend to underperform; a softer-than-feared message would relieve them. *(Framework, not prediction.)*
- **Growth vs. value:** A "higher-for-longer" confirmation pressures long-duration growth/tech valuations more than value; persistent ~4.4%+ 10Y yields raise the discount rate on far-out cash flows. Commentators broadly frame mega-cap tech as the swing factor for index-level moves.
