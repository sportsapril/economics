- Net read: the Fed's stated bias (dot plot, Warsh, Waller) still points toward hike risk later in 2026, but the market has concluded a July 29 hike specifically is very unlikely, with the more "live" debate shifting to the September 16–17 meeting. This is a real tension, not a resolved one — worth watching for whether Warsh's July 29 press conference reaffirms or walks back the hawkish framing given the recent soft data.
